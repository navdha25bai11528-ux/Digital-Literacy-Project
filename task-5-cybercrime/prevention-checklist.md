# Prevention Checklist
1. Use strong and unique passwords for all accounts
2. Never share OTPs, passwords, or banking details with anyone
3. Always check the URL before entering login information
4. Avoid clicking on suspicious links in emails or messages
5. Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible
6. Do not use public Wi-Fi for financial transactions
7. Regularly update your apps and devices for security patches
8. Verify unknown calls or messages claiming to be from banks or services
9. Double-check UPI IDs before sending money
10. Do not approve unknown payment requests
11. Report at: cybercrime.gov.in
12. Helpline: 1930
